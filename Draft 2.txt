1st chapter of Act 2:
Seralina and Nyara arrive in Dreamfield and are amazed at the beauty of the scenery and the vastness of the expanse, but are disturbed at the arrival of the Advocates of will.
The advocates take Seralina and Nyara to the collective led by Nami. Everyone talks about mythological beings about and about Jachar, which confuses her. It turns out they speak of Kavel.
